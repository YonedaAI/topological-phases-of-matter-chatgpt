# Paper 1 Brief: Locality

## Research question

When does a compact, profinite, or condensed parameter family of local quantum interactions admit thermodynamic dynamics and Lieb-Robinson estimates uniform in the parameter?

## Strongest defensible target

Prove a family version of established Lieb-Robinson and thermodynamic-limit results under a common interaction-norm bound and local parameter continuity. Show that condensed descent organizes the family but supplies none of the estimates.

## Dependencies

- Admissible metric lattice and F-function.
- Uniform interaction Banach-norm bound.
- Local continuity with uniform tail control.

## Deliverables and gates

- LaTeX paper and current PDF of at least 20 pages.
- Strict Haskell demonstrations for finite envelopes and counterexamples.
- Lean theorem-provider interface with explicit analytic hypotheses.
- Primary-source audit and AGY publishability review.

## Nonclaims

- Profinite parameters do not imply uniform locality.
- Lieb-Robinson bounds do not imply a spectral gap.
- Pointwise gappedness does not imply a uniform gap.
