# Paper 5 Brief: Physical Realizability

## Research question

Which abstract invertible bordism or homotopy classes have explicit local, symmetric, uniformly gapped microscopic representatives?

## Strongest defensible target

Separate Freed-Hopkins field-theory classification from Kubota's microscopic Omega-spectrum, then verify a portfolio of low-dimensional representatives such as the Kitaev, BDI, and AKLT chains. Record all remaining surjectivity questions as open unless a theorem gives an obstruction.

## Dependencies

- Papers 1 through 3 define a microscopic phase.
- Paper 4 defines comparison arrows and their known limits.
- A fixed distinction between spatial dimension and spacetime dimension.

## Deliverables and gates

- LaTeX paper and current PDF of at least 20 pages.
- Strict Haskell realizability registry and witness checks.
- Lean stacking, invertibility, and relative-charge interfaces.
- Primary-source audit and AGY publishability review.

## Nonclaims

- Freed-Hopkins is not a lattice-realization theorem.
- Kubota's spectrum is not yet a condensed or bordism comparison theorem.
- No known model means `OPEN`, not `OBSTRUCTED`.
